Support

Vo-Cal · we read every message

Vo-Cal is a voice-first nutrition tracker: tap, say what you ate, and it builds your log. If something isn't working or you have a question, reach out and a real person will help.

Contact

Email support@vo-cal.app. We aim to reply within two business days. Include your device model and iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Common questions

How do I log a meal? Tap the + button, pick a meal type, and say what you ate in plain language. Vo-Cal transcribes it, breaks it into items, and estimates calories and macros. You can edit anything before you confirm.

How do I delete my account? Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all your data.

Is this medical advice? No. Vo-Cal provides nutrition information for educational purposes and is not medical advice.
