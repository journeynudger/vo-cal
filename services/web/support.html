Frequently asked questions

What does Vo-Cal do?
Vo-Cal turns spoken meals into a tracked food log. Tap the add button, pick a meal type, and say what you ate in plain language. Vo-Cal records your voice only during that moment, transcribes it, breaks it into items, and estimates your calories and macros. You can review and edit everything before you confirm.

How do I delete my account and data?
Open Settings → Delete account in the app and confirm. This permanently and irreversibly removes your account and all of your data — your voice recordings, your food log, your check-ins, your plan, and your profile. It cannot be undone, and signing up again starts you from a clean slate. See our Privacy Policy for the full list of what's deleted.

How do I reach support?
Email the address above. Including your device model, iOS version, and what you were doing when the issue happened lets us reproduce it quickly.

Is Vo-Cal medical advice?
No. Vo-Cal provides nutrition information for educational purposes only and is not medical advice. Talk to a qualified professional before making significant changes to your diet.
