Privacy Policy

Last updated 25 June 2026

Vo-Cal is a voice-first nutrition tracker: you say what you ate, and the app turns it into your food log. This policy explains what we collect, why, where it lives, who can see it, and the control you have over it. We keep it short because the app keeps it small — no ads, no trackers, and we never sell your data.

What we collect

  • Voice recordings — captured only while you are logging a meal. The audio is the source of truth for your log, so we keep it to power transcription and to keep an accurate record of what you said.
  • Health & fitness inputs — your meals and macros, the intake answers used to build your plan, and the bodyweight and activity you enter during intake and weekly check-ins.
  • Account identifier — Vo-Cal uses Sign in with Apple. This gives us an account identifier and may include an email address, which is often Apple's private relay address rather than your real email.
  • First-party usage metrics — basic in-app events (such as durations, counts, and confidence levels) that we generate ourselves to keep the app reliable. These contain no phone numbers and no precise health values.
We do not collect your location, contacts, browsing history, or financial information.

How we use it

Only to make the app work for you: turning your voice into a food log, computing your targets, showing your progress, and keeping the app stable. We do not use your data to track you across other apps or websites, and there are no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in the app.

Where it lives

Your data is stored in our cloud database and file storage (hosted on Supabase), scoped to your account. Voice recordings are kept as the immutable ground truth for your log — your transcript and the parsed food items are derived from that audio. To process a logging request, your audio is sent to our transcription provider (ElevenLabs) and your resulting transcript to our parsing provider. These providers act only on the data you send to fulfill that request; they are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

Who can see it — beta review disclosure

Vo-Cal is currently in a private, invite-only beta. During this beta, our team may review your data — including your voice recordings, transcripts, and food log — to improve the app's transcription and parsing accuracy. We state this plainly so there are no surprises. This admin access is limited to our team and is logged. We expect to narrow or remove this review access as the app matures, and we will update this policy when we do.

Your control & deletion rights

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account inside the app. This sends a request to our server (DELETE /account) that permanently and irreversibly removes your data: your voice recordings, your captures, transcripts and parses, your meals and corrections, your check-ins, your saved meals, your intake answers and plan, your profile, and the usage metrics tied to your account — and finally your account identity itself. Once deleted it cannot be recovered, and signing up again starts you from a clean slate.

Children

Vo-Cal is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Contact
