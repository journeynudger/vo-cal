Privacy Policy

Vo-Cal · last updated 2026

Vo-Cal is a voice-first nutrition tracker. This policy explains what we collect, why, where it lives, and the control you have over it. We keep it short because the app keeps it small: no ads, no trackers, no selling your data.

What we collect

  • Voice recordings you make while logging a meal. Audio is the source of truth, so we keep it to power transcription and to improve accuracy.
  • Your food log and nutrition data - meals, macros, and the intake answers used to build your plan.
  • Bodyweight and activity you enter during intake and weekly check-ins.
  • Your account identifier (Sign in with Apple, or an anonymous account). If you use Sign in with Apple, this may include an email address, often Apple's private relay.
We do not collect your location, contacts, browsing history, or financial information.

How we use it

Only to make the app work for you: turning your voice into a food log, computing your targets, and showing your progress. We do not use your data to track you across other apps or websites, and there are no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in the app.

Where it lives & who can see it

Your data is stored in our cloud database and file storage, scoped to your account. Voice audio is sent to our transcription provider (ElevenLabs) and your transcript to our parsing provider solely to process that request. During our early concierge beta, our team may review your data to improve the app's accuracy - we mention this plainly so there are no surprises.

Your control

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, your food log, your voice recordings, and your plan. It cannot be undone.

Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@vo-cal.app (see also our Support page).
